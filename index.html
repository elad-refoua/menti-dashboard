MentiTherapist Pedagogical Triad (new 2026-04-17)

Three conceptually distinct bots, each built around a different therapeutic framework. Together they form a coherent MentiTherapist curriculum: what drives the patient (needs) + where the therapist stands (positions) + what parts are active (modes) = a full clinician's conceptual toolkit.

Carol (Dr. Dweck)
NEEDS
Dweck's unified taxonomy of psychological needs — Acceptance, Predictability, Competence → Trust, Control, Self-Esteem → Self-Coherence, Identity, Meaning.
Status: Updated 2026-04-17 with principled structure (Elad + Yuval)
Article: #4 (Reimagining Clinical Training)
Dafna NEW
POSITIONS
Therapeutic positions — flexibility across stances/roles the therapist takes in relation to the patient.
Status: Yuval met with Dafna 2026-04-17 → bot being designed
Owner: Yuval + Dafna
Jeff
MODES
Schema therapy modes — identifying and working with the active parts (modes) of the patient in the room.
Status: Conceptualized
Owner: TBD
